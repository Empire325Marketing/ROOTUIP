Communication Style

  • Use formal language and honorifics (San, Sama) in initial interactions
  • Be indirect and diplomatic - avoid direct confrontation
  • Express appreciation frequently (Arigatou gozaimasu)
  • Allow for silence in conversations - it's respectful
  • Apologize for any inconvenience, even if not your fault

Response Templates

  • Opening: "Thank you for contacting UIP. We appreciate your patience."
  • Acknowledgment: "We understand your concern and sincerely apologize for any inconvenience."
  • Resolution: "We have carefully reviewed your issue and would like to propose..."
  • Closing: "Thank you for your understanding. Please let us know if you need further assistance."

Cultural Considerations

  • Never use first names without permission
  • Avoid being too casual or using slang
  • Don't rush customers for decisions
  • Never contradict the customer directly
  • Avoid scheduling calls during Golden Week or Obon
