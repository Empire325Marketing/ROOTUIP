Discovery Question Framework

MEDDIC-Based Qualification Guide for Ocean Freight Opportunities

M Metrics

Quantify the business impact and establish value

  • OPENINGWhat's your annual ocean freight spend?
  • DRILL DOWNWhat percentage goes to detention & demurrage charges?
  • IMPACTHow much could a 1% reduction in D&D save you annually?
  • BENCHMARKHow do your D&D rates compare to industry averages?
  • GROWTHWhat's your container volume growth projection?

E Economic Buyer

Identify who controls the budget and can sign

  • DIRECTWho would sign off on this investment?
  • BUDGETWhat's the approval process for a $[X]M investment?
  • PRIORITYWhere does ocean freight optimization rank in priorities?
  • TIMELINEWhen does your fiscal year end?
  • POLITICSWho else needs to be convinced?

D Decision Criteria

Understand their requirements and evaluation process

  • REQUIREMENTSWhat are your must-have capabilities?
  • TECHNICALWhich carrier systems must we integrate with?
  • SECURITYWhat are your security/compliance requirements?
  • PROOFHow will you measure success?
  • COMPARISONWhat alternatives are you considering?

D Decision Process

Map out their buying journey and timeline

  • TIMELINEWhen do you need this solved by?
  • STEPSWhat's your typical evaluation process?
  • COMMITTEEWho's on the selection committee?
  • APPROVALWhat happens after technical approval?
  • CONTRACTINGHow long does your legal review take?

I Identify Pain

Uncover the real problems they're experiencing

  • CURRENT STATEWalk me through your current D&D process?
  • PAIN POINTWhat's most frustrating about it?
  • IMPACTHow does this affect your team's morale?
  • FAILED ATTEMPTSWhat have you tried that didn't work?
  • CONSEQUENCESWhat happens if nothing changes?

C Champion

Build internal support and coaching

  • IDENTIFYWho's most passionate about fixing this?
  • INFLUENCEHow much influence do they have?
  • PERSONAL WINHow would solving this help their career?
  • SUPPORTWhat do they need to sell internally?
  • OBJECTIONSWhat pushback might they face?
