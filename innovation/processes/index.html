Innovation Pipeline Stages

1

Ideation

Capture and collect ideas from all sources - employees, customers, partners, and market research.

🕒 1-2 weeks 👥 5-10 people
2

Evaluation

Score ideas based on impact, feasibility, alignment, and resource requirements.

🕒 1 week 👥 3-5 people
3

Prototype

Build minimal viable prototype to test core assumptions and technical feasibility.

🕒 2-4 weeks 👥 2-4 people
4

Validation

Test with users, gather feedback, and validate market fit and business value.

🕒 3-6 weeks 👥 4-8 people
5

Scale

Develop full solution, integrate with systems, and roll out to broader audience.

🕒 3-6 months 👥 10+ people

Proof of Concept Development

Week 1

Discovery & Planning

  • Define success criteria and KPIs
  • Identify key assumptions to test
  • Assemble cross-functional team
  • Create technical architecture draft
Week 2-3

Rapid Prototyping

  • Build core functionality MVP
  • Create basic UI/UX mockups
  • Implement critical integrations
  • Setup testing environment
Week 4

Testing & Validation

  • Conduct user testing sessions
  • Measure performance metrics
  • Validate technical assumptions
  • Gather stakeholder feedback
Week 5

Analysis & Decision

  • Compile results and learnings
  • Calculate ROI projections
  • Present to steering committee
  • Make go/no-go decision

Validation Methods

👥

User Testing

Direct feedback from target users through interviews, usability tests, and beta programs.

⏱️ 2-4 weeks
👤 20-50 users
📊

A/B Testing

Compare variations to determine optimal solutions through controlled experiments.

⏱️ 2-6 weeks
📈 95% confidence
🎯

Market Testing

Limited market release to validate demand, pricing, and operational readiness.

⏱️ 4-12 weeks
🌍 1-3 markets
🔬

Technical Validation

Performance testing, security audits, and scalability assessments.

⏱️ 1-3 weeks
✓ Pass/Fail
💰

Business Case

Financial modeling, ROI calculations, and total cost of ownership analysis.

⏱️ 1-2 weeks
💵 3-year NPV
🤝

Partner Validation

Feedback from key partners, suppliers, and ecosystem stakeholders.

⏱️ 2-4 weeks
🏢 5-10 partners

Recent Lessons Learned

Blockchain Shipping Platform

Technology

Overestimated market readiness for blockchain. Partners not willing to share data on distributed ledger.

Pivoted to private consortium model with selective data sharing.

AI Customer Service Bot

Product

Underestimated complexity of shipping-specific language and regulations in different regions.

Implemented hybrid model with human escalation for complex queries.

Mobile-First Platform

Market

Enterprise customers still preferred desktop for complex operations despite mobile trend.

Developed responsive design prioritizing desktop power users.
