Voice & Tone

UIP speaks with authority and confidence while remaining approachable and solution-focused.

✓ Do

  • Lead with concrete ROI numbers
  • Use active, confident language
  • Focus on business outcomes
  • Be specific about capabilities
  • Emphasize speed and efficiency

✗ Don't

  • Use vague marketing speak
  • Over-promise capabilities
  • Focus on features over benefits
  • Use overly technical jargon
  • Sound desperate or salesy

Messaging Examples

Hero Headline: "Stop Losing $14M Per Vessel to Ocean Freight Inefficiencies"

Value Prop: "Universal integration hub connecting 200+ carriers in 48 hours"

CTA: "Calculate Your D&D Losses" / "See ROI in 30 Days"

Visual Style Principles

Design Principles

  • Clean & Professional: White space, clear hierarchy, no clutter
  • Data-Forward: Visualizations and metrics take center stage
  • Trust Indicators: Security badges, uptime stats, customer logos
  • Action-Oriented: Clear CTAs, obvious next steps
  • Responsive: Works perfectly on all enterprise devices
